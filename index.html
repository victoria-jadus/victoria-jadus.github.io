Selected Projects

Scalp-Mounted OCT Imaging Device

Designed and prototyped a scalp-mounted fixture for an OCT imaging system using SOLIDWORKS. The design supported an approximately 8 lb system for clinical-style use and included consideration of user needs, manufacturability, and device stability.

3D Endometrial Organoid Research

Supported development and maintenance of 3D organoid cultures, including optimization of growth conditions, passaging, reproducibility troubleshooting, and protocol development for future lab use.

Decellularized ECM Hydrogel Protocol

Worked on protocol development involving enzymatic digestion, buffer preparation, sterile processing considerations, and experimental planning for tissue-derived hydrogel applications.

Neonatal Incubator Design Project

Developed a prototype incubator concept using Arduino-based temperature and humidity monitoring, calibration planning, and engineering design documentation.
